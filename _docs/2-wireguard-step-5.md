After creating a client:

- Click **Download** to get the `.conf` file.
- Or click **QR Code** to scan with your mobile WireGuard app.

![Created Client](https://tomeksdev.com/assets/img/post/wireguard/created-client.png)

Apply the config to your device and start the VPN tunnel.
